About Me

Born and raised in Perth - my passion has always been within the IT and Web sector. Witnessing the growth of technology throughout the early 21st century have prompted me to follow this wave. I have worked at the Engineering Institute of Technology for over five years now - a business that offers online and on-campus engineering courses in both the Vocational and Higher Education sector. Our institute manages and maintains our information systems primarily in-house; chiefly as our requirements as a dual-sector organisation requires considerable customisation.

My experiences have yielded the following: technology is logical - people are sometimes not. Project management and business process improvement has proved to be taxing at times, but my belief is that approaching all aspects of work as objectively as possible mitigates unhelpful conflict and justifies decisions.
